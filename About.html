Sassy Longtail at Fashion Week 2009

Weiner Dog Boutique was launched fifteen years ago in East Texas by Sassy Longtail with the introduction of her direct to consumer on-line dog hat boutique, Wuf Coture. As the world's first weiner dog milner, she filled a gap in the fashion world that had long been ignored by the mass market. Her first hat, "The Floppy", debuted at Fashion Week 2009. It was launched as a ready to wear line at Target as a seasonal winter product. The Floppy set records as the most popular dog Chritmas gift. Since her unprecidented success, Sassy's mission has been to continue to reflect the tastes of weiner dogs everywhere while including a taste of East Texas flair in every piece. Unmistakable details of antique lace, ruffles and bright colors remain her signature style. With the creation of the online boutique, Sassy directs the focus to other up and coming weiner dog designers who are influencing a whole new generation of fashion.
